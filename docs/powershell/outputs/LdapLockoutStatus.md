### LdapLockoutStatus
LDAP lockout status.

- isLocked: System.Boolean
  - Whether the principal is locked out.
- lockReason: LdapLockReason
  - Lockout reason.
- unlockReason: LdapUnlockReason
  - Unlock reason.
- lockedAt: DateTime
  - Lockout timestamp.
- unlockedAt: DateTime
  - Unlock timestamp.
